Your Home Plan

Sattva SimpliCity, Bengaluru, is an upcoming residential landmark in the thriving neighborhood of RR Nagar, Southwest Bengaluru. Spread across 9 acres, the development comprises 3 elegant towers rising 22 floors high, offering over 700 thoughtfully designed 1, 2, and 3 BHK apartments ranging from 550 sq. ft. to 1,800 sq. ft. Inspired by the philosophy that “simplicity is the ultimate sophistication,” the project blends refined design with modern comforts. With well-planned homes, premium finishes, and world-class amenities, Sattva SimpliCity promises a lifestyle that is graceful, contemporary, and effortlessly convenient — true to its name
